About the Project

The Spaceflight News project is a web application designed to provide users with up-to-date information about spaceflight events, missions, and news. Our goal is to make it easier for enthusiasts, researchers, and the general public to stay informed about developments in the space industry.

What to Expect

On this platform, users can explore the latest articles from Spaceflight News, save their favorite articles for future reference, and view a curated list of trending topics. The application offers a seamless user experience, with responsive design and a robust backend to support real-time data fetching and storage.

Addressing the Information Problem

With the rapid growth of the space industry, there is an overwhelming amount of information scattered across various platforms. This project aims to centralize spaceflight-related news in one convenient location, helping users quickly access relevant and accurate information without sifting through multiple sources.
